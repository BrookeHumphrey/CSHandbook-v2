Overview

The Athena AI module supports providers in generating, reviewing, and signing clinical notes within AthenaNet. This guide covers initial configuration, usage tips, and troubleshooting steps.

1. Setup & Access

  • Confirm that AthenaNet integration is active for your site.
  • Access Athena AI via the Documentation tab in the EHR menu.
  • Enable AI note generation under Provider Settings → AI Tools.

2. Using Athena AI in Notes

Once configured, Athena AI assists with auto-populating structured text in the SOAP format. Providers can dictate findings and review AI-generated summaries before signing.

  • Dictate: Record patient interactions as usual.
  • Review: Check AI-suggested findings for accuracy.
  • Sign Off: Finalize the note to save it to the patient chart.

3. Troubleshooting

If Athena AI content does not load or fails to apply, check the following:

  • Confirm that the AI Note Generation setting is turned on.
  • Ensure that dictation permissions are enabled for the logged-in provider.
  • Verify stable network connectivity.

For persistent issues, contact the Customer Success Team with the provider’s username, encounter date, and error message.

4. Best Practices

  • Review every AI-generated note before signing.
  • Use structured macros consistently for clear and compliant documentation.
  • Report inaccurate or incomplete AI outputs to support for review.
